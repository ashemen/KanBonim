# Content Audit

## Executive Summary

Kan Bonim already has a strong foundation: a clear mission, a memorable core idea, credible founders, defined services, testimonials, contact details, and content around dating, matchmaking, religious-Zionist singles, community responsibility, and emotional readiness.

The current Wix site feels sincere and content-rich, but the structure is thin. Many pages are short, calls to action are inconsistent, SEO metadata and heading hierarchy can be improved, and the site does not yet fully guide each visitor type toward the right next step.

The rebuild should keep the warmth, faith-based language, and professional sensitivity, while making the site clearer, more trustworthy, easier to scan on mobile, and stronger for Hebrew search.

### Homepage

Current purpose:

- Introduces the brand idea: a good match is not accidental; it is built through awareness, emotional work, listening, and responsibility.
- Presents five services.
- Introduces Ariel and Nechama Shemen.
- Shows testimonials.
- Promotes a survey.
- Promotes the podcast.
- Lists address, phones, email, and social links.

Strong material:

- "כאן לא מחכים - כאן בונים" is a strong brand line.
- The metaphor of building a Jewish home is emotionally and culturally suitable.
- The homepage has real credibility: bios, testimonials, services, and contact information.

Weaknesses:

- The first screen should more quickly explain who the service is for and what action to take.
- Service cards are useful, but they need clearer audience labels and next actions.
- Testimonials need stronger presentation and light editing for spelling/clarity while preserving authenticity.
- Contact details exist, but there is no prominent conversion path such as WhatsApp, phone, or short form.
- Podcast and survey feel appended instead of strategically integrated.

## Content Issues To Fix

- Correct typos and grammar while keeping authentic Hebrew voice.

  - רווקים ורווקות
  - דתי-לאומי or דתי לאומי, consistently
  - שדכנות אפקטיבית
- Fix the lectures page opening summary, which currently describes matchmaking rather than lectures.
- Remove or update outdated event text on the community training page.
- Avoid generic Wix phrases such as "Service Information".
- Replace unclear "לפרטים" repetition with page-specific CTAs.
- Improve testimonials for readability, with permission if editing heavily.
